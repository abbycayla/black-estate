DAMSTEEP VINEYARD

Damsteep Vineyard (formally Spye) is
located in the North East of Waioara Valley at the
foot of the Omihi Saddle, 10km North of our
Home Vineyard. Riesling and Pinor Noir grow here
with vine density of more than 5000 vine per hectare.

Pinot Noir is grown on a 5 hectare North East facing
slope, with an alititude between 140 to 180 meters
above sea level. The vines here were planted in
1999 and grow on clay overlaying fractured
limestone and sandstone soils. The Reisling Vineyard
is 2.2ha and lies on a North facing slope below
the Pinot Noir. The soil is in very dense/brown/orange
clay and inter bedded with sandstones and subordinate
limestone horizons.
