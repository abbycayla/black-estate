Black Estate is a remarkable
place for wine.

We're set on making our wines true to the
place they grow. The wine making is simple-
good estate grown fruit, organic and biodynamic
hand-picked, vineyard derived yeasts, no additives
or fining, minimal sulfur at bottling and
where possible, no filtration. Then we have character.
